steak

ingredient

  • Beef
  • unsalted butter
  • salt
  • thyme leaves
  • black pepper
  1. Season: Pat steak dry and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat pan: Preheat a skillet (preferably cast iron) over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook: Add butter. Cook steak for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  4. Rest: Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
