mac and cheese

  • elbow macaroni
  • shredded cheese (cheddar is best, but you can mix)
  • 2 cups milk
  • butter
  • flour
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Cook pasta: Boil macaroni, drain.
  2. Make sauce: Melt 2 tbsp butter, stir in 2 tbsp flour, add 2 cups milk, whisk until thick.
  3. Add cheese: Stir in 2 cups shredded cheese until melted. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Combine: Mix pasta with cheese sauce. Serve!
